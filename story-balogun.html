Mr. & Mrs. Balogun

The couple who stood in the gap when it mattered most

“What they did was small to them, but it was everything to me.”

My Lifeline During the Storm

Mr. and Mrs. Balogun may never fully understand what they did for me. When I stood on the edge of giving up, they stepped in — quietly, generously, and wholeheartedly. They didn’t ask for anything in return. They didn’t make a show of it. They simply saw a young student in need… and gave.

When I couldn’t afford to sit for my WAEC exam — the test that would determine my future — they paid for it. That act may have taken just a moment for them, but it changed the entire direction of my life. Their belief in me carried a weight I’ll never forget.

Because of them, I found the strength to keep going. I walked into that exam hall not just with a pen, but with the silent encouragement of two people who believed I was worth investing in. And because of that, I now walk a path where I can help others — just as they helped me.

This tribute is for the unsung acts of kindness that ripple through generations. Thank you, Mr. & Mrs. Balogun. You are a part of my story forever.
